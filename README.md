# Focus
Focus is a simple, clean, and distraction-free writing app designed to help your thoughts flow without interruption—no ads, no servers, no clutter. Everything is stored locally on your device, and you can save, edit, and download your writings as Markdown files. Whether you enjoy the nostalgic click of a typewriter (optional), prefer dark mode, or write in bold, italic, and underlined styles, Focus adapts to your needs—supporting both left-to-right and right-to-left languages for a seamless writing experience in any script.
