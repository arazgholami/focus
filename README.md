# Focus
Focus is a simple, clean, and distraction-free writing app designed to help your thoughts flow without interruption—no ads, no servers, no clutter. Everything is stored locally on your device, and you can even save, edit, and download your writings as Markdown files. Whether you prefer a bright screen or dark mode, or enjoy the nostalgic click of a typewriter (which you can turn off anytime), Focus offers just the right tools—supporting bold, italic, and underlined text—to let you write your way, your pace.
